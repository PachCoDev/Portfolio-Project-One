My Experience

Experience:

To give you some more background about me, I started my career as a sales rep at 29prime. As much as I loved sales, I wanted to be more involved with the product. I always wanted to be able to explain to customers exactly how things work on top of how it can benefit them if they were interested.

My dad was working at MindFire, Inc. at the time and let me know they were hiring. As soon as I saw they were a technical company I jumped at the opportunity. I couldn't believe it when I was offered a job interview.

After long discussion, I was hired by MindFire, Inc. on April 13, 2015! I will never forget that day as it was the best day of my life! I have been working at MindFire ever since and plan to stay as well as improve on my skills while I am there for the better of the company and myself.
